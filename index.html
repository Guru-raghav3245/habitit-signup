How to Join

Step 1: Join the tester group

Open Google Group

After opening the page, click the "Join group" button.

Step 2: Become a tester

Open Play Store Page

On the page, click "Become a tester" and accept the invitation.

Step 3: Install the app

Once accepted, click "Install" from the Play Store.

Make sure you are logged into the same Google account for both steps.

If the app is not visible, wait a few minutes and try again.
